Apple is a global technology company focused on premium consumer electronics and ecosystem services.

Key Products:
- iPhone (major revenue contributor)
- Mac and iPad
- Apple Watch and wearables
- Services like iCloud, Apple Music, App Store

Revenue Model:
- Hardware sales (primary)
- Subscription services (growing segment)

Market Position:
Apple is positioned in the premium segment with strong brand loyalty and ecosystem lock-in.

Recent Strategy:
- Expanding AI features in devices
- Increasing services revenue
- Focus on privacy and security

Competitors:
Samsung, Google, Microsoft
