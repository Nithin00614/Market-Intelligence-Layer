[SECTION: COMPANY_OVERVIEW]
Apple is a global technology company focused on premium consumer electronics and ecosystem services.

[SECTION: PRODUCTS]
iPhone is Apple's primary revenue driver.
Mac and iPad serve productivity markets.
Wearables include Apple Watch and AirPods.

[SECTION: REVENUE]
Revenue comes from:
- Hardware sales (major portion)
- Services like iCloud, App Store, Apple Music (fast-growing)

[SECTION: STRATEGY]
Apple focuses on:
- AI integration in devices
- Expanding services ecosystem
- Privacy and security differentiation

[SECTION: RISKS]
- Supply chain dependency (China)
- Regulatory pressure
- High product pricing limits market expansion

[SECTION: OPPORTUNITIES]
- AI-powered devices
- Services growth
- Expansion in emerging markets

[SECTION: COMPETITION]
Samsung, Google, Microsoft
